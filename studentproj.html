Project Overview

For this project I used a dataset from Kaggle about student habits such as amount of sleep, study hours per day, and exercise per day and compared them to their academic performance. The question was to see if there is a relationship between the amount of study hours and exam scores.

Text Analysis Example

After cleaning the data, finding the average scores and study hours, I made code to see if students who studied for more than 5 hours a day have higher scores on average compared to students who had studied less than 5 hours.
